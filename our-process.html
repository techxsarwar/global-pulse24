Our Application of Media Integrity

How GlobalPulse24 curates reliable, bite-sized news for the next generation.

01

The Selection

We scour dozens of high-authority global sources (Reuters, AP, BBC) to identify stories with significant global impact. We prioritize factual reporting over opinionated commentary.

02

The Summary

Our student editors distill complex articles into concise, 4-paragraph summaries. The goal is to make news "bite-sized" and accessible without losing critical context or nuance.

03

The Ethics

We do not use AI to generate "fake news". Every summary is fact-checked against the original source. We strictly credit original publishers and claim no ownership of the primary reporting.
